About SuperFokus

Introduction: SuperFokus is a versatile productivity tool designed to help you stay concentrated, manage your time, and minimize distractions. Built on core principles of focused work sessions, it provides three distinct "Fokus Modes" that you can configure to perfectly match your workflow and personal habits.

Repeating Reminders: The "original" mode of Fokus. It involves a popup that continuously flashes on your screen after a set amount of minutes and seconds. You can configure custom messages and define the exact number of reminder rounds.

Pomo Style: A structured time-management mode that functions similarly to Repeating Reminders, but incorporates scheduled breaks. You can configure dynamic work and break sequences, and either receive a gentle reminder or have the application completely block your screen to enforce downtime.

Site Blocker: A powerful tool to keep you away from digital distractions. You can create lists of specific domains or URLs to either block completely or exclusively allow, ensuring your browser remains a tool for work rather than procrastination.

Sidebar Features: The robust sidebar gives you access to real-time productivity statistics, a visual heatmap of your activity, comprehensive session history logs, and customization options to tailor themes and audio notifications to your liking.

Conclusion: Together, these features make SuperFokus not just a simple timer, but a comprehensive environment for mastering your attention. Whether you are studying, coding, or just trying to get through the day's tasks, SuperFokus is here to keep you on track.
